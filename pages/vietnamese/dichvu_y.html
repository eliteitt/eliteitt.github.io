Why choose ELITE ITT
for all your (Vietnamese and English) Medical Interpreting and Translating needs?

If you are a specialist or a cosmetic surgeon, you may not have access to a free interpreter either onsite or over the phone. It is recommended that you always have a professional interpreter & translator present when your non-English speaking patients sign any document.

Our Principal Interpreter and Translator - Dr Giao Quynh Tran - has extensive international experience in medical, legal medical and allied health interpreting as well as translation. With her at the forefront, ELITE ITT’s medical interpreting and translating services have exceeded client expectations.

ELITE ITT is here to ensure effective communication between medical practitioners, psychologists, allied health professionals and patients with limited English proficiency. We can help you avoid communication problems which may cost you more than a little down the track. Doctors, specialists, psychologists, nurses, allied health professionals and patients have found our interpreting the most reliable.
