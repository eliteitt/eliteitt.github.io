Thông Dịch Điện Thoại và Video

We provide quality Phone Interpreting and Video Interpreting services in all areas of expertise both locally and internationally. The Principal Interpreter - Dr Giao Quynh Tran - is substantially experienced in national and international phone interpreting as well as audio visual link (AVL) interpreting. Contact us before your next phone / video conference, and rest assured that you will have the most productive one possible.

Why choose ELITE ITT
for all your (English and Vietnamese) phone interpreting needs?

In the digital world nowadays, communication is borderless. Wherever you are, connect to us and your ideas or messages will be made pleasantly comprehensible to your English / Vietnamese business partners, clients, etc. Our Australian clients have appreciated and complimented a lot on our helping them achieve good deals with their Vietnamese business partners.

Moreover, whenever you encounter any unforeseen language barrier no matter how big or small in your daily life, at work, at home or overseas, you can always count on us. Simply give us a call. We will gladly assist you.

ELITE ITT's audio visual conference interpreting quality is assured by Dr Giao Quynh Tran, who has dozens of years of experience interpreting in teleconferences and AVL conferences in the U.S. and Australia as well as has received a lot of positive customer feedback. We always strive to provide the best phone and video interpreting services across disciplines nationwide and worldwide. Our audio visual conference interpreting expertise, our good customer service and our client satisfaction have been some of our trademarks.
