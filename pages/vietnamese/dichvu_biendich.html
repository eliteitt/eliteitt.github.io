Why choose ELITE ITT
for all your (English into Vietnamese and Vietnamese into English) Translating needs?

At ELITE ITT, we have competent translators whose accurate and artistic translations have been widely recognized. The Principal Translator - Dr Giao (Grace) Quynh Tran - is well-versed in translation for news and journalism. She translated for several years for Vietnamese newspapers and magazines such as "Thanh Nien" Daily ("The Young People"), "Kien Thuc Ngay Nay" Biweekly ("Knowledge for Nowadays"), "The Gioi Moi" Biweekly ("The New World"), "Phu Nu Ap Bac" Monthly ("Ap Bac Women"), etc.

Moreover, Dr Tran is a prolific legal, medical, technical, business, community and literature professional translator, having provided numerous quality translations of legal and medical documents, technology documents and forms, brochures, manuals, leaflets, stories, etc. She has also provided well-written TV program subtitles in Australia and beautiful voice-overs in government-funded documentaries in the U.S. In addition, she is a respected teacher of interpreting and translation.

From standard to technical documents and from subtitles to voice-over projects, our work quality has been unbeatable. We always invest a lot in our translations, subtitles and voice-over projects to make sure that they are the best possible.
