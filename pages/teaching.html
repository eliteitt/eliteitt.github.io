Interpreting & Translation Teaching AND NAATI Certification Training

We specialise in training candidates to sit the NAATI Level 2 (paraprofessional) and Level 3 (professional) tests, teaching legal / medical interpreting and teaching translation in English and Vietnamese, both language directions. Our lessons have been considered the most helpful and interesting by our students.

More importantly, we train, we use. The best students from our courses, after passing the NAATI Level 3/ Professional interpreting tests, will be trained further and used by us. Our training and accordingly your progress go beyond the NAATI Level 3 tests and continue thereafter until you are ready to be selected for work by Elite ITT.

The Principal Teacher, Dr Giao (Grace) Quynh Tran, has a strong academic background and extensive experience in teaching. Her dedication to and creativity in teaching have made her a prominent teacher in interpreting / translation in the Vietnamese and English languages at Western Sydney University and at Elite ITT. She also received great feedback from attendants at her AUSIT Court Interpreting workshop.

A good teacher is also a strong researcher. Dr Tran has carried out a lot of fruitful research projects resulting in publications in Applied Linguistics, TESOL and Court Interpreting. Based on peer review, she is doing a great job supervising MA theses in TESOL at Western Sydney University.

With Dr Tran being the lead teacher, there is no doubt about the teaching quality at Elite ITT. Here students have the opportunity to learn from the best in the interpreting and translation profession to hopefully become one someday.

If you speak both English and Vietnamese and have pondered becoming an interpreter / translator, do not wait any longer. Enroll at Elite ITT today.
